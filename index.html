What is Freestyle Football?

Freestyle Football(known as Freestyle Soccer in the U.S) has been around for decades. A man named Mr.Woo from South Korea was the first person to perform freestyle football tricks in front of a crowd of spectators. A youtube video of his performance in 1988 can be found here.
During the early 2000's, a commercial of Ronaldinho performing soccer tricks was aired on Dutch television. The video captured the attention of many teenagers and children. Kids starting going outside with their footballs and tried to mimic the same tricks they saw on T.V.
Now, in the year 2015, the level of freestyle football has increased dramatically and new tricks are being discovered each day!
Events like the Redbull Street Style competition have begun to emerge, and winners will forever have their names immortalized in the record books.
How big will freestyle football be within the next few years? We'll only have to wait and find out. Until then, grab your football, and start practicing!
